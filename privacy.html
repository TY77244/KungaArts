Ibijanye n'Ibwiriza ryo Kurinda Imyirondoro (Privacy Policy)

Kuri KungaArts, umutekano w'amakuru yawe n'imyirondoro yawe n'uburenganzira bw'ibanze. Iri bwiriza rirasobanura neza uko twakira, uko tubika, n'uko turinda amakuru watanze igihe twandikaga profil yawe cyangwa ukoresha icyumba cy'ibiganiro (chat room).

1. Amakuru Twakira n'Icyo Ayakoreshwa

Igihe wiyandikisha cyangwa ukoresha uru rubuga, twakira amakuru akurikira:

  • Imyirondoro y'ibanze: Amazina yawe, igitsina, n'ikiciro cy'ubuhanzi ukoramo (kugira ngo ugene amatsinda akubereye).
  • Aho uherereye (Address): Intara, Akarere, Umurenge, Akagari, n'Umudugudu (kugira ngo dushyire abahanzi mu matsinda hakurikijwe uturere batuyemo).
  • Ihuza n'Umutekano: Numero ya WhatsApp, imiyoboro y'imbuga nkoranyambaga (nk'imyiyoboro ya YouTube), n'ifoto ya profil yawe.
  • Numero y'Indangamuntu (National ID): Iyi mibare 16 yakirwa gusa mu rwego rwo kwemeza ko uri umuntu muzima kandi urinda umutekano mu muryango w'abahanzi (isuzumwa rimwe gusa).

2. Uburyo Amakuru Yawe Abikwamo (Data Storage)

Amakuru yose yongewe mu mafomu n'ubutumwa bwo mu cyumba cy'ibiganiro bibikwa mu buryo bwakwiriye hakoreshejwe ububiko bwa mudasizira (Local Storage/Databases) bukurikije amategeko y'ikoranabuhanga mu Rwanda. Ntabwo dusangiza cyangwa ngo tugurishirize amakuru yawe ibigo by'ubucuruzi bby'abandi bantu batari KungaArts.

3. Uburenganzira Bwawe ku Makuru Yawe

Gukurikiza Itegeko rya Rwanda ryo kurinda amakuru bwite (Law N° 058/2021), ufite uburenganzira bukurikira:

  • Kureba amakuru yawe yose twabitse igihe cyose ubyfuje.
  • Guhindura cyangwa gukosora imyirondoro yawe igihe yahindutse (bifashishije urupapuro rwo kwiyandikisha).
  • Gusaba ko amakuru yawe n'ubutumwa bwawe bikurwa mu bubiko bwacu burundu (Right to be Forgotten).

4. Guhinduka k'Iri Bwiriza

KungaArts ishobora kuvugurura iri bwiriza igihe cyose bibaye ngombwa mu rwego rwo gukurikiza amategeko mashya y'igihugu cyangwa kongeramo ibiranga sisitemu nshya. Ibyahindutse bizajya bimenyeshwa abakoresha uru rubuga binyuze ku rupapuro rw'Ahabanza cyangwa mu cyumba cy'ibiganiro.

5. Twandikire

Niba ufite ikibazo, icyizere, cyangwa icyifuzo bijyanye n'uko amakuru yawe akoreshwa, ushobora kutwandikira binyuze ku buyobozi bwa KungaArts cyangwa ukabaza mu cyumba cy'ubutumwa cy'itsinda ryanjye.
